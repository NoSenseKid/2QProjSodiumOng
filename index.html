About Pokémon Unite

About This Guide

In this guide, I will help you learn more about roles and held items in the game. Roles are the use and purpose of each Pokémon in a game. It gives us an idea of its strength and weaknesses. Held Items are things that can increase your Pokémon’s strength like giving more damage, health points, or movement speed and only a maximum of 3 held items can be placed in one Pokémon. The part where it gets difficult is when you choose your roles and held items and how you play with it. That is why I created Unite Lab and Stuff, my website about Pokémon Unite, to help you overcome the difficulties and make you a better player.

Benefits of the Guide

To be more knowledgeable of the game.

To help you win your games using basic strategies.

To adjust your role to the game.

About Me

Hello there trainers! I am Gian Ong, the one who created this guide. I'm just a high school student who likes to play video games. I also have experience in playing mobile games especially Mobile Legends. When Pokemon Unite was first released in mobile, I loved the game but the ranking system has a flaw. There are newbies in high rank games which ruins the fun for those who wants to solo rank. It also made me think that most players that started Pokemon Unite don't really play MOBA games before. I want to help make the game more fun by giving newbies some game knowledge and a school project just been announced back then. I realized that this should be my topic and this is how I started this guide.
