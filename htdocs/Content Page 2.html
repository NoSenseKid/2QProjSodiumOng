Different kinds of Playstyles

Passive

Passive is a playstyle where you prioritize yourself from not dying over anything. This playstyle is focused on being defensive against enemies.

Aggressive

Aggresive is a playstyle where you prioritize on killing an enemy while staying alive in the end. This playstyle is focused on being offensive against enemies.

Balanced

Balanced is a playstyle where you try to pressure enemies while also defending yourself. This playstyle is focused on finding opportunities that will help you win the game.

Finding your “Perfect Role” with regards to your playstyle

Passive

Go with Pokemon that have weak damage in the early part but strong damage in the late part of the game.

Aggressive

Go with Pokemon that have a strong damage in the early part of the game to utilize it. You can also use Pokemon that initiates a fight.

Balanced

Go with Pokemon that can maintain their good damage throughout the entire game. You can also use Pokemon that initiates a fight or Pokemon that supports teammates from fights.
